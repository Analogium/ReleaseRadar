About the Release

"Ethereal Synthesis" marks Prism Theory's fourth studio album, exploring the intersection between organic soundscapes and algorithmic textures. Recorded over eighteen months in a secluded mountain studio, the record captures the silence of the peaks and the hum of the machine.

Visual Concepts

The visual identity for this era was designed by Neural Flow Collective, utilizing real-time data from electromagnetic fields to generate the fluid distortions seen in the cover artwork.
